How Certification Works

A real standard, not a sticker.

01

Apply

A place tells us who they are and what they already do for animals. Two minutes, no fee.

02

Assess

PFA reviews the place against a published checklist - a visit where it matters, a conversation where it helps.

03

Certify

Meet the standard and you earn a tier - Certified, Gold or Platinum - plus the PFA X mark and a public listing.

04

Renew

The mark is reviewed each year and can be reported by anyone, so it always means something.

No tier can be bought, at any price. Certified is the standard. Gold needs twelve months held with no upheld report. Platinum needs thirty-six. There is no fee that shortens this and no one at PFA who can waive it.

Received

A reviewer at PFA checks your registration against the issuing authority. If it holds, an admin decides whether this place goes on the register. Neither step is automatic and neither is instant.

Being listed is not the mark. Once you are on the register, people near you can attest to what you have claimed here. The mark follows from them, not from us.
