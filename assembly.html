The reason for all of this

The animals are the work.
The people are the reason.

A PFA Assembly is not an event. It is where a person who has been feeding fifteen dogs alone on a street finally meets seven others who have been doing the same, three streets away. It is where a twenty-two-year-old who wants to talk about the ABC Rules discovers she has one hundred and forty listeners. It is where a person who cares about animals stops being alone.

Come to one. Bring the next.

The Guardrails

Ten rules. No exceptions.

The Assembly works because every assembly obeys these. Read them once. If any of them is a problem for what you want to do, this is not the place for it.

Guardrail 01

It must be for the animals.

Not for you. Not for your business. Not for your politics. Not for your religion. Not for a fundraiser that routes to someone else. Nothing served, sold or handed out is animal-based - no meat, fish, eggs, dairy, leather, silk or wool. An assembly for the animals does not consume them. If the animals are not the reason, this is not the place.

Guardrail 02

No cruelty. No illegality.

No performances using animals. No baiting, dyeing, dressing or handling for spectacle. No content or actions that break the law. Any Assembly that crosses this line ends immediately, and the organiser is banned.

Guardrail 03

Safety first. For the animals. For you.

First aid on site. A vet on call. Insurance for rallies over one hundred people. Adult supervision if minors are present. If your assembly involves handling an animal, a trained volunteer is present.

Guardrail 04

Consent for every face.

If minors are on camera, their guardians sign off in writing. If an animal is in distress, no camera in its face. Anyone in the room can ask to not be photographed and the answer is yes.

Guardrail 05

No doxxing. No public PIN codes.

If you name a feeder, get their permission first. If you name a cruelty offender, work with the police, not the crowd. No home addresses, no exact locations of vulnerable animals or people.

Guardrail 06

No solicitation through this channel.

You cannot use The Assembly to route donations to yourself or a third party. All monetary asks go through PFA India, and only PFA. No QR codes, no personal UPI IDs, no third-party payment links on stage.

Guardrail 07

One organiser is the organiser.

That person is eighteen or older. If younger, a named adult guardian co-signs. That person is on the ground on the day. That person is responsible for what happens. No anonymous assemblies.

Guardrail 08

Reviewed by your nearest unit.

Every Assembly is reviewed by the PFA unit in your city before it is published. They know the ground. They can help you plan it. They can also say no. Their word is final.

Guardrail 09

No political campaigning.

No election candidates on stage. No party branding. No caste or religious appeals. This is a place for animal welfare, and only animal welfare. The animals are the only party here.

Guardrail 10

Be kind. No harassment, no hate.

Every language is welcome. Every faith is welcome. Every person who cares is welcome, whatever their politics outside this room. Anyone who breaks this rule is banned from the Assembly for life.

The Guardrails are non-negotiable. Assemblies that break them are taken down within the hour. Organisers who break them are barred from the Assembly, permanently. This is how we keep it a place worth coming to.
