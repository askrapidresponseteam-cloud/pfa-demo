This month · Assignment 01

File the FIR.

Learn to walk into a police station and file a First Information Report when you see an animal being harmed. Three steps. Twenty minutes. When you finish, you have done something most people never will.

  1. Step 01
    Learn the three sentences
    The exact wording, in plain Hindi and English, that gets an FIR registered under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act.
  2. Step 02
    Download the one-pager
    Fits in a wallet. Section numbers, your rights, the escalation path if the SHO refuses.
  3. Step 03
    Log that you know it
    A quick check. You are now part of the map of people in India ready to walk into a station.
