Step Four

The caretaker's undertaking.

The card comes with responsibilities recognised under the ABC Rules. Read them, and give your word.

Colony
Identity

As a caretaker, I undertake

  • To feed responsibly, at fixed points and times, keeping the area clean.
  • To support the sterilisation and vaccination of the animals I care for, under the ABC programme.
  • To not obstruct lawful ABC operations, and to help catchers safely when they come.
  • To treat every animal without cruelty, and to resolve conflict with neighbours calmly and lawfully.
  • To carry this card as identification, not as authority over public spaces or other people.
